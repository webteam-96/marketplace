FAQ

Common questions, honest answers.

We have 3 different accounts and want funds in all three accounts

Provide three bank account details and the proportions you want each to receive — the system will automatically distribute every collected amount across them.

Some members pay annual and some bi-annual. Will this work?

Yes. Members who have paid their complete dues are automatically removed from the reminder list. Both annual and bi-annual cycles are supported.

What about members who pay by cheque or cash?

Manually update their record once you've received the payment — they will then stop receiving reminders, just like online payers.

2.5% is very high charges. Club cannot afford this amount.

The 2.5% is a non-negotiable platform/payment-gateway cost. However, the total savings — fewer uncollected dues, no bank trips, no manual chasing — far exceed what clubs typically lose to manual processes.

How will this system enable 100% Collection?

Unpaid members continue to receive scheduled reminders, and pending names are visible to the treasurer in a drop-down — making it easy to follow up on the few that remain. Most clubs report reaching 100% collection within a few cycles.
